Cabbage Roll Casserole

Description

My cabbage roll casserole has all the ingredients of stuffed cabbage rolls but is a lot easier to make!
This basic recipe will appeal to everyone but if you prefer more flavor, consider adding crushed garlic,
paprika, thyme, or cayenne pepper.

Incredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef

  • 3 Â½ pounds chopped cabbage

  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce

  • 1 cup chopped onion

  • 1 cup uncooked white rice

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Combine cabbage, tomato sauce, onion, rice, and salt in a large mixing bowl.
    Stir in cooked ground beef. Pour mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish, then pour
    beef broth over top.

  3. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Stir, re-cover, and bake until
    cabbage is tender and rice is done, 20 to 30 minutes more.
